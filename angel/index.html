ARTIST STATEMENT

My work exists in the liminal space between presence and absence, exploring the architectural qualities of light and the sculptural nature of shadow. Through minimal compositions, I seek to distill reality to its essential forms.

Each photograph is an investigation into the tension between the seen and unseen, the constructed and the natural, drawing inspiration from brutalist architecture and the philosophical frameworks of negative space.
