Professional Background

I'm a commercial real estate broker with a passion for learning about biotech research and innovation. As a board member and Treasurer of ChiTownBio—Chicago's first community biotech lab—I lead operations while learning firsthand how labs function and how scientists approach problems.

At Matanky Realty Group, I'm immersed in the commercial real estate world, supporting deals that have generated over $1M in asset value growth and $5M+ in retail property sales. My technical background (Computer Science and Economics, RIT '19) brings a unique perspective to the team—from building financial models to optimizing our CRM systems.

What excites me most is operating at the intersection of different worlds: applying tech skills to real estate, bringing business acumen to biotech, and helping build communities where innovation thrives.
